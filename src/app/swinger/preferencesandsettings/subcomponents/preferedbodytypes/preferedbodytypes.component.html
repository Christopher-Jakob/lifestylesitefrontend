Preferred Body Types
Body Type Filtering is an optional filter that requires additional verification. In order to filter members by their body types your body type must be verified. Body Type Verification ensures quality matches for those who have a body type preference. You have the option to allow those who have not opted into body type filtering to still see and contact you based on other filters as well as you to see and contact them. However those who have opted into body type filtering and do not meet your filter requirements will not be able to see or interact with you, or you them. Body type verification must be re verified every three months, but can be re verified as many times as you like before then. Body type verification is taken in the same way your initial verification was taken, a picture of you holding a sign with a six digit number covering your face. In body type verification it is requested that you show as much skin as you are comfortable. You do not have to be nude but the more we see of your body, the easier it is to accurately determine your body type. Each member in a couple will have a body type classification.
Body Type Classifications Below
Body Builder
Member spends a great deal of time and effort into their fitness, making it a high priority in their life. Very low body fat percentage, with a high amount of muscle.
Athletic
Member spends a significant amount of time on their fitness but not to the intense degree as the body builder. Member has a low but not very low body fat percentage with a high to significant amount of muscle.
Average
Member spends some to little time on their fitness. Members have some to low amount of muscle, with a greater than low, but not significant amount of body fat.
A Few Extra Pounds
Member has a significant amount of body fat with very little to low muscle mass.
Heavy Set
Majority amount of body mass is fat, with very little to low muscle mass.
